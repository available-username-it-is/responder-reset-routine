Responder-controlled sharing

For a Finland/EU-friendly demo, individual off-duty biometrics stay private by default. The responder can choose whether to share a limited signal or export a report.

Product principles

Finland-oriented language

Position as recovery, work ability, and occupational safety: palautuminen, työkyky, and työturvallisuus. Avoid “mental health risk scoring” or “stress surveillance.”
